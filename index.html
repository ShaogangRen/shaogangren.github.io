Shaogang Ren

Email: renshaogang [at] gmail [dot] com

I'm a postdoctoral researcher working with Prof. Xiaoning Qian at the ECE department and TAMIDS, Texas A&M University.

My research interests include deep generative models, representation learning, causal discovery, and their applications in medical data analysis, computer vision, etc. I also have worked on optimization methods to scale up the solutions of sparse learning.
